Humanity has without a doubt entered a new era of technological innovation. In order to prepare students for an ever modernizing world, American High School offers courses in engineering and computer science to inspire them to pursue careers in STEM. Thanks to sponsorships and support from Lam Research, Toyota Corporation, Cargill Corporation, ARM, and Mission Valley ROP, American High School has been able to expand to offer two main pathways to build foundations in computer science and engineering.
